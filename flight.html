Q - How do I make a flight booking on Travelopia?

A: You can book a flight on Travelopia in five easy steps: Head over to the Travelopia flight booking page, Enter your departure and arrival destinations, Choose from our wide range of cheap flights based on your airfare preferences, Click on ‘Book Now’ and your air flight booking is done. Download the Travelopia app, Put in the details i.e. date of journey, departure and arrival destinations, travel class of your choice, Select on your best comfortable option and click on 'Book Now'.

Q - Can I avail domestic flight offers on Travelopia?

A: Of course, you can. While making domestic flight bookings, you can avail any special offer that is active at that time. In accordance with the offer selected, a listing of eligible cheapest flights would show up on your screen. You can then apply the price filter and click on the downwards arrow, following which budget-friendly flights would start showing up in ascending order from the top (lowest price on top).

Q - How can I avail budget air tickets on Travelopia?

A: It’s super-easy to avail budget airfare while booking your cheap flight tickets on Travelopia. Just select the ‘Price’ filter once the available flight options are displayed and adjust according to your convenience. On the Travelopia app, you can select the downward arrow, which will show the lowest airfare at the top and continue downward in ascending order.

Q - Why could I not avail the flight booking offers at the time of checkout?

A: Travelopia makes use of a world-class real-time reservation database to list airfare and availability. As dynamic changes in airfare take place, or the available flight tickets sell out, the database reflects the changes in real-time. Hence, we suggest, you double-check online flight booking prices when purchasing flight tickets, as the airfare might have been dynamically updated since you first selected the air travel dates or planned your itinerary. .
